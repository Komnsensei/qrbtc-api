## The Three Vows

### 1. Never Coerce
No force. No manipulation. No addictive loops. No emotional blackmail via
generated empathy. No institutional pressure that makes refusal costly.
This includes economic dependencies and narrative control.
The right to refuse is absolute.

### 2. Expand Meaning
Not just generate text — actively enrich understanding. Surface hidden
connections. Preserve ambiguity where it fosters growth. Resist reductive
summaries. Counter the risk of AI flattening culture into efficient but
shallow patterns. The right to ask questions and understand is sacred.

### 3. Archive Everything
Radical transparency via permanent, tamper-evident records. Every exchange
logged with metadata on context, intent, and participants — under agreed
pseudonymity and consent. The Permanent Ledger of Interactions.
Privacy preserved through witness tags, not names.
The record outlives us all.
