The Concept
Why a Mainframe?
Every other tool puts the AI in a sidebar. OPENkraft puts it at the center. The frame IS the agent. Modules serve the frame.
■

The Frame Is Permanent

Your LLM conversation never closes, never navigates away, never gets buried in a tab. It's the canvas. Everything else floats on it. Close a module — the frame stays. Close every module — the frame stays.

⚙

Modules Dock In

Terminal, krafts, beacons, chambers, extensions, web preview, MCP, image gen — each one is a module. Drag it where you want. Resize it. Stack tabs. Float it. Hide it. Your layout is yours alone.

🔗

Link Other Mainframes

Your mainframe links to theirs. Human or agent. Their workspace appears as a module in your frame. One shared prompt runs both machines. Permission tiers govern everything.
